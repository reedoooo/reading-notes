Measuring Emotional Intelligence

  • I recently completed an Emotional Intelligence survey, and I found the results quite insightful. It appears that I possess a strong aptitude for recognizing and comprehending my own emotions, enabling me to effectively manage them in positive ways. This heightened self-awareness proves beneficial in problem-solving and stress management scenarios. Moreover, the survey indicated that I exhibit good interpersonal skills, facilitating smoother collaborations with others. Overall, the results indicate a satisfactory level of emotional intelligence, while acknowledging that there is always room for further improvement. This survey has served as a valuable tool to evaluate my emotional intelligence and has provided motivation to continue nurturing this important aspect of personal growth.

Bias

  • According to the results of the Bias Self-Assessment survey, I have gained valuable insights into my biases and areas for growth. The assessment revealed that I exhibit a high level of self awareness, curiosity and courage. I realize I must be mindful of these biases and work towards addressing them. Despite the results, I still have a need for ongoing self-reflection and conscious efforts to challenge and mitigate biases that may affect my interactions with others. This survey has provided a valuable opportunity to assess and further develop my awareness of biases, contributing to a more inclusive and unbiased approach in my interactions and relationships.
